The CEO of one of the largest companies in the world is going to enlighten the students about what it takes to be a good leader. Leadership is the ability to guide and influence others towards achieving a common goal. Effective leaders possess a range of qualities, including strong communication, decision-making, and problem-solving skills. They inspire and motivate their team members, foster collaboration, and exhibit integrity and resilience. In a discussion about leadership, topics may include the importance of vision, empathy, adaptability, and continuous learning for successful leadership.
